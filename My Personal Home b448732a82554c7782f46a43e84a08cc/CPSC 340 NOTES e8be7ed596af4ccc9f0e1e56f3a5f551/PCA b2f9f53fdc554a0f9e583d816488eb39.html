Data Centering

The data is centered if each column of X has a mean of zero.

It is easy to center the data:

set μj=1n∑i=1nxij\mu_j =\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^nx_{ij}μj​=n1​∑i=1n​xij​﻿ (mean of column 'j')

Choosing ‘k’ by “Variance Explained”

For a given k, we have a ratio: ∣∣ZW−X∣∣F2∣∣X∣∣F2\frac{||ZW-X||_F^2}{||X||_F^2}∣∣X∣∣F2​∣∣ZW−X∣∣F2​​﻿

The variance explained is 1-ratio

  • If you want to “explain 90% of variance”, choose smallest ‘k’ where ratio is < 0.10.
